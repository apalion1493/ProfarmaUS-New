What is a Cooke?

When you visit profarma-direct.com, cookies are stored on your computer. A cookie is a small text file placed in your browser to recognize your computer on subsequent visits. Our cookies do not store personal information.
Almost all websites use cookies. They are used to help analyze how the website is used, aiming to improve the user experience. In many cases, cookies are necessary to provide certain services on the website.


The information in your cookies is transmitted between your browser and a web server, containing details about user settings, login, and website usage.
Some cookies are temporary and expire at the end of the user session when the browser is closed (session cookies). Others are stored on your device for a longer period (persistent cookies). When you revisit a website, new session cookies are placed in temporary memory, while persistent cookies are updated.



Your Rights

As an individual, you have the right to be forgotten according to the GDPR. This means you have the right to have your data deleted 'without undue delay'. If you wish to delete your personal information, we will remove all information that we are not legally required to retain.

Personal data pertaining to potential new customers is collected only after obtaining consent and is deleted upon withdrawal of that consent. Data pertaining to former customers is deleted 5 years after the end of the customer relationship, but not before the resolution of any pending cases or fulfillment of any outstanding obligations (e.g., warranty claims).
